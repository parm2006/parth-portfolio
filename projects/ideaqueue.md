# IdeaQueue

**Status:** Concept / redesign planned

Productivity app concept for quickly capturing ideas and surfacing them later by priority.

## Concept

IdeaQueue is designed as a lightweight, cross-device idea inbox. Users can add thoughts quickly, store them in a database, and later review the highest-priority item from a queue.

## Planned Features

- Fast mobile-friendly idea capture.
- Database-backed persistence.
- Priority queue behavior.
- Categories and filters.
- Review or "pop next idea" workflow.
